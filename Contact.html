About Us

In 2024, three students—Kainan Wyatt, Jeremiah Morgan, and Leuel Tesfaye—were brought together for a common goal: to create the best website possible. And thus, Cooking Delights was born! This recipe website was crafted with accessibility in mind, incorporating numerous modern features that combine the best elements of other cooking platforms. With a shared aspiration for excellence in Web Development, these three Lipscomb students embarked on their journey. Despite the challenges they faced along the way, they persevered and finally unveiled the finished product—the very site you are viewing now. So, without further ado, we invite you to discover the joy of cooking with Cooking Delights!
